Since it is BBQ theme'd. Collect the BBQ sauce through out the world. Then go back and share it with the guests by talking to them.
This happens once you have collected 5 BBQ sauce which will trigger and event turning you into able to help the guests.

After you talk to 6 guests when you have 5 BBQ sauce which you will have "helped" you will get a you win pop up.
